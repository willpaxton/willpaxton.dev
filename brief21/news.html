News Updates

2021 Focus

January 3rd, 2021 | by John Johnson

This year, our organization is focusing on products to help us end this pandemic so we can get back to normal. We will be returning to a normal production cycle at some point this year, reversing from our temporary production boost on our REMOTE line and producing products like normal. Thank you for your patience during these times. We are still here if you need anything.

Covid-19 Update

February 5th, 2022 | by John Johnson

Hello, with the upcoming health crisis starting in China and slowly spreading, we are adapting our business to better suit you during these times. We are increasing production on products that tailor to remote services, like our REMOTE line. We are also increasing our support hours to better suit you in these difficult times. As always, we will be innovating to create products that specifically tailor to this time. Thank you.

REMOTE line introduced

August 24, 2019 | by John Johnson

Hello! Today we are happy to announce a new line of products from medikom. Our new REMOTE line is targeted to those who want to keep monitoring their patients from their home environments. We have plans to create a blood pressure monitor, a glucose monitor, and a fall sensor for this new line. If you have any suggestions, you can contact us here with what you would like to see added. Thank you for your continued support.

New Product: Telemedicine Kiosk

September 28, 2018 | by John Johnson

Today, I am proud to announce our new product, telemedicine kiosks. These kiosks are custom designed for your needs. These devices are designed for more rural locations to allow medical offices to have more outreach into communities with need. These are still in an early prototyping phase, so they are not available to purchase just yet. Check in often for more information!
